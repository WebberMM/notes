* Introduction to Vectors
  #+ATTR_LATEX: :options []
  #+BEGIN_definition
  The *dot product* or *inner product* of \(\bv=(v_1,v_2)\) and \(\bw=(w_1,w_2)\) is
  the number \(\bv\cdot\bw\)
  \begin{equation*}
  \bv\cdot\bw=v_1w_1+v_2w_2
  \end{equation*}
  #+END_definition
